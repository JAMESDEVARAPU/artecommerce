Build a clean, elegant, and visually rich website for an Art Educator and Handmade Art Business.

Business Overview

The owner is an art educator who creates handmade arts, home décor items, customized gifts, and also conducts art classes and workshops (online and offline).

The website must showcase artwork, sell products, manage orders, and allow students to register for classes and workshops.

Website Goals

Showcase handmade art, décor, and gift items professionally

Accept and manage product orders (ready-made + custom orders)

Promote and manage art classes and workshops

Build trust and credibility as an art educator

1. Home Page

Elegant hero section with tagline and call-to-action buttons

Sections for:

Handmade Art & Décor

Art Classes

Workshops

Testimonials section

Gallery preview

Call-to-action: “Shop Now”, “Join a Class”

2. About Page

Artist/educator story

Teaching philosophy

Experience and achievements

Professional yet warm tone

3. Gallery / Portfolio

Categories: Handmade Décor, Gifts, Paintings, Crafts

High-quality image grid

Short descriptions for each artwork

4. Shop / Orders Page

Product listings with images, price, description

Ready-made products (Add to Cart)

Custom order request form

Order summary and confirmation

5. Art Classes Page

Class types (Kids, Adults, Beginners, Advanced)

Online and Offline options

Registration form with payment option

6. Workshops Page

Upcoming workshops with date, time, venue

Seat availability

Registration + payment

Past workshop gallery

7. Contact Page

Contact form

WhatsApp button

Email and social media links

Location (if offline classes exist)
